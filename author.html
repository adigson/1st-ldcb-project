My lineage

  • From Ko'ole: A Traditional Paedeatrics from Ile-Ife

    He was my great grand father. He journeyed to Abeokuta where he was welcomed and made to feel at home due to his feats and thus established himself at Ita-Elega in Abeokuta.

  • Bello Bolaji: My Grand Father

    This was the first to accept Islaam in my lineage from Ko'ole.This he did while at Abeokuta and remained therein till death.

  • Muhammad Murtado Bolaji (Apola): My Father

    Late Apola breath his last in the year 1994 at his residence in Ibadan, Oyo State. He was survived with two house-wives (one of whom is my mum), children and grand-children. In fact, I am the 14th of his children.

  • Muhammad Misbaahud-Deen: Myself!

    I was born on the 20th of March, 1980 in the city of Ibadan at a Catholic Hospital. Left for Lagos in 1984 wherein I did my primary and secondary schoolings. I graduated from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Osun State in 2007 with a 2.2 B.Sc Honors in International Relations. Though I married while in Ile-Ife during my 300 level years, I am residing in Warri Delta State at the moment. This was where I carried out my national service. Equally, my secondary place of assignment during my NYSC was at a Madrasah with which the desire to stay back after service was gathered. Al-hamduliLlaah this has continued to provide notable opportunities at propogating the Deen of Allaah!

As at the 7th of July 2020, my Immediate Family is a product of seven members... al-hamduliLlaahi 'alaa Kul-li haal!
